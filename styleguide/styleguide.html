Site Purpose

Recipes from the corner cupboard of my kitchen.

Target Audience

25-45 year old women in the United States. The average income is $50,000 per year. Visitors have some college. Married. Homemaker. They may have a part-time job of 20 hours or less per week. They use the web everyday, mostly on their phone.

Typography

The fonts are contrasting. The fonts should coordinate with the logo fonts, yet be websafe and easy to read. I chose a handwriting font for Headings because the recipes on the site should have a handwritten feel. I chose a sans serif font for the body copy because the actual recipes should be easy to read.
